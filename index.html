Meemobar for macOS

Meemobar

Clipboard history text manager for macOS

Meemobar is a lightweight menu bar clipboard text manager that automatically saves everything you copy and lets you quickly search, reuse and organize your clipboard history.

Available for macOS 14.6 or newer

Clipboard History
Automatically stores copied text so you can access it anytime

Protected Items
Keep frequently used snippets at the top of your clipboard

Automatic Clean up
Set how long clipboard items are stored and keep your history organized

Open at Login
Seamlessly integrated into the macOS menu bar for easy access

Built for macOS

Native macOS high-performance experience.
Fast, lightweight and designed to speed up your workflow without getting in the way.
Perfect for developers, writers, designers and anyone who copies and pastes throughout the day.

Theme Options
Choose Light, Dark, or System Default to match your macOS appearance

Detailed Item
Extended view for text items in clipboard history that stays on your Mac
